Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the TikTok Posting Assistant (“the Service”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you connect your TikTok account and use the Service.

1. Information We Collect

  • Your TikTok account ID (open_id)
  • Access tokens granted through TikTok OAuth
  • Video metadata required for posting (file size, caption, etc.)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information solely to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account
  • Upload videos on your behalf
  • Publish posts using TikTok’s Content Posting API

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect passwords
  • We do NOT collect private TikTok messages
  • We do NOT sell or share your data with advertisers or third parties

4. Data Storage & Security

Tokens may be stored securely for posting automation. We implement reasonable security measures, but no system is 100% secure.

5. Revoking Permissions

You can revoke the Service’s access at any time through TikTok’s "Manage App Permissions" settings.

6. Children’s Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under 13.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the Service implies acceptance of any changes.

8. Contact
